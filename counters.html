Methodology

Counter performance on this page is evaluated using a consistent simulation standard to ensure fair comparison across all raid bosses.

⚔️ Level 40 counters
🌩️ Extreme weather conditions
🤝 No friendship bonus
👥 No party power boost
🔥 Hardest boss moveset selected
⏱️ Performance measured using TTW

Results are displayed as Time to Win (TTW) and the percentage difference compared to the best-performing counter.
